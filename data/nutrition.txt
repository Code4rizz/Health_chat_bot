Nutrition plays a vital role in maintaining overall health and well-being.
A balanced diet provides the body with essential nutrients needed for energy, growth, and repair.

Eating a variety of fruits and vegetables helps supply vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.
Whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats support heart health and muscle function.

Good nutrition helps strengthen the immune system and reduces the risk of chronic diseases.
Poor dietary habits may lead to fatigue, weakened immunity, and increased health risks.

Proper nutrition works together with regular exercise, adequate sleep, and hydration to maintain a healthy lifestyle.
